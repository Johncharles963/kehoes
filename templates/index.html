About Us!

Kehoe’s is a family owned and operated jewelry store located in the beautiful Flathead valley of northwest Montana. The shop is a unique blend of a fine jewelry store and a rock shop. It was started in the early 1930’s by Jack Kehoe, a man with enough vision and determination to establish a business making jewelry and cutting Montana agate during the height of the great depression.

From the past to the present, this tradition continues today. Kehoe’s still sells Montana agate along with beautiful jewelry in gold, platinum and sterling silver, all set with colorful gemstones. We specialize in Montana’s famous cornflower blue Yogo sapphire, as well as, the sapphire found in the Philipsburg area. A wide variety of natural stone carvings, petrified wood bookends, minerals and fossils from around the world can also be found in the store. We hope you can visit us in Montana one day soon.
